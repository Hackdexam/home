WHY E-LEARNING?

Although the world is suffering from global pandemic and countries are going through economic crisis, a lot of industrial sectors have been concluded but at the same time e-learning is the platform which is reshaping and growing rapidly. From the first day of announcement of Lock down the nationwide usage of internet has increased by 47 % during the period, It is clear that people are getting more reliable over internet, instead of watching televisions people likes to watch Netflix and so-on. Since, it was impossible to continue with the traditional method of education during the global pandemic, a new-form of teaching came into existence or more clearly people interacts with the new form of education which is "E-learning".
Now, the question arrives Why E-learning?
 Since global pandemic is the foundation of digital education but this can not only be the reason to keep up e-learning, the following are the reason listed.

  • Maximize Knowledge Retention
  • Better Understanding
  • Simplified knowledge
  • Availability of material
  • Increase Productivity
  • Cost Reduction
